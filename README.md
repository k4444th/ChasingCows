# Chasing Cows

Chasing Cows is a single player casual game. The story begins with an apocalypse of murder-grass on your home (alien) planet. In a forgotten library you find the answer to your planet's problems. Only one kind of animal in the known universes can help you save your planet from being overrun by alien-eating flora.
You steal the last working starship on your planet and make your way across the galaxy to steal and bring back as many murder-grass eating animals (cows) as you can.
But the murder-grass is nifty and keeps evolving. Will you be able to save your planet before the forces of unified planets put a stop to you?
